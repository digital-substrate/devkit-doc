Ecosystem
=========

A technical orientation across the dsviper ecosystem. Use this section to find
your way through the components: which one does what, what depends on what, and
where to start depending on what you are trying to build.

Components are designed so each can stand on its own, but they compose into two
value chains that together form the full DevKit experience. If you are new,
start with :doc:`value-chains` — it is the map.

For marketing positioning, product comparisons, and use cases, see the website
at https://digitalsubstrate.io. This section is the *technical* counterpart:
how the pieces are wired and where to enter depending on your intent.
